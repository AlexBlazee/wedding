Our Story

For years, we both lived within the same world — connected together by family, time, and a quiet sense of familiarity. We knew of each other… but life followed its own timeline.

Until one day — the conversation finally began.

What followed were moments,

and meaning into something undeniable.

With the support of both families —
Hanumaiah & Hymavathi and Venkateswara Rao & Lakshmi Kumari —
who had, in their own way, always held this story close,

Yes.

Here we are Megha & Pradeep, with our families —
inviting you to celebrate our wedding and share in this special beginning with us.
